Shift Workout Tracker

Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 19, 2026

Shift ("we", "our", or "the app") is a workout tracking app built by Zuhayr Khan. This policy explains what data Shift collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

The short version: Your data is yours. We do not sell it, share it with advertisers, or use it for any purpose other than making the app work for you. We use no analytics or tracking SDKs.

1. Data We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Password (securely hashed; we never see or store your plaintext password)

If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive only the identity token needed to authenticate you. We do not access your Apple or Google account data.

Profile Information

You may optionally provide:

  • Name
  • Age
  • Body weight
  • Profile picture

Workout Data

Shift stores the workout data you create within the app:

  • Workout plans and exercises
  • Workout sessions, sets, reps, and weights
  • Exercise goals and progress
  • Custom exercises you create

Body & Progress Tracking

  • Weight entries (manual logs)
  • Body measurements (e.g., chest, waist, arms)
  • Progress photos (uploaded to secure cloud storage)

Apple HealthKit Data

With your permission, Shift reads the following from Apple Health:

  • Step count
  • Walking + running distance
  • Active energy burned (calories)
  • Exercise minutes
  • Stand hours
  • Heart rate
  • Body weight
  • Workout records
  • Activity ring summaries

Shift may also write the following to Apple Health:

  • Completed workout sessions
  • Body weight entries
HealthKit data is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising. This data is used solely to display your activity within the app, power goal tracking, and sync workouts between Shift and Apple Health.

Voice Input

Shift uses on-device speech recognition to let you describe your workout preferences to the AI plan generator. Voice audio is processed entirely on your device using Apple's Speech framework and is never sent to our servers or any third party.

On-Device AI

The AI plan generator uses Apple's on-device Foundation Models framework. All AI processing happens locally on your device. No workout data, preferences, or prompts are sent to external AI services.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Provide the core app functionality (tracking workouts, plans, and goals)
  • Sync your data across your devices
  • Display activity and progress information
  • Send local notifications about your goals (if enabled)
  • Generate AI-powered workout plans on your device

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond app functionality.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Local storage: Your data is stored locally on your device in an encrypted SQLite database.
  • Cloud sync: Data is synced to Supabase, a secure cloud platform, using encrypted connections (TLS/HTTPS). Row-level security ensures you can only access your own data.
  • Progress photos: Stored in Supabase Storage with access restricted to your account.
  • Authentication: Managed by Supabase Auth with industry-standard security practices.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with any third parties. Specifically:

  • No data is sold to data brokers or advertisers
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs are used
  • No data is shared with marketing platforms
  • HealthKit data is never shared with third parties

The only third-party service we use is Supabase for secure data storage and authentication.

5. Your Rights & Controls

  • Access: All your data is visible to you within the app at all times.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account at any time from Settings. This permanently removes all your data from both the device and our servers, including workout history, profile information, and progress photos.
  • HealthKit: You can revoke Shift's access to Health data at any time via iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Health > Shift.
  • Notifications: You can disable notifications in the app settings or via iOS Settings.
  • Photo protection: Progress photos can be locked behind Face ID in app settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Shift is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
